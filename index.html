Our Story

Rooted in Rajasthan.
Built on Trust.

"Every slab tells the story of the earth — we simply help it find its home."

Hargobind Marble was born in the heart of Kishangarh — India's marble capital — where the craft of working with natural stone is a way of life passed down through generations. For over three decades, we have supplied the finest marble and natural stones to architects, interior designers, builders, and homeowners across the country.

Our legacy is built on a simple belief: that the beauty hidden within each stone deserves to be honoured. From pristine white Makrana marble to rich green serpentines and golden-veined exotics, our collection spans the full spectrum of nature's geological artistry.

Our Heritage

The Kishangarh Legacy

Kishangarh has been the marble hub of India for centuries. Nestled in the Aravalli hills of Rajasthan, its quarries yield some of the most exquisite natural stones in the world.

01

The Land of Marble

Kishangarh sits atop one of India's richest marble belts. The region's unique geological composition produces stones of unmatched quality — from pure white Makrana to rich pink and green varieties prized by royalty for centuries.

02

Generations of Craft

Hargobind Marble was founded on knowledge passed down through generations. Our team of master craftsmen understand the nuances of each variety — its grain, veining, strength, and ideal application.

03

Modern Excellence

Today, we combine our deep-rooted heritage with modern processing technology — precision cutting, polishing, and quality control systems that bring out the natural brilliance of every slab.
